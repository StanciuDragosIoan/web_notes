Factorial

In algorithm analysis and Big O notation, O(N!) represents an algorithm or problem with a time complexity that grows factorialy with the size of the input. "N!" stands for "n factorial," which is the product of all positive integers from 1 to n.

In Math the Factorial of a number represents the product of all positive integers lass than or equal to N: 

      5! = 5 x 4 x 3 x 2 x 1 = 120
      4! = 4 x 3 x 2 x 1 = 24
